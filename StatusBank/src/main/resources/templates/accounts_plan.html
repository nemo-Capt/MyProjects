Accounts plan

Account number Account type Description
51 Settlement accounts Account 51 "Settlement accounts" is intended to summarize information on the availability and movement of funds in Belarusian rubles on the settlement accounts of the organization opened with banks.
55 Special bank accounts Account 55 “Special accounts in banks” is intended to summarize information on the availability and movement of funds in Belarusian rubles and foreign currencies held on special accounts in banks, as well as the presence and movement of precious metals and (or) precious stones in bank accounts ( hereinafter - accounts in precious metals)
55-1 Deposit accounts On the sub-account 55-1 "Deposit accounts" the presence and movement of funds placed in bank deposits (deposits) are taken into account
55-2 Precious metal accounts On subaccount 55-2 "Accounts in precious metals" the presence and movement of precious metals and (or) precious stones in bank accounts are taken into account.
55-3 Special purpose cash account On the sub-account 55-3 "Special Purpose Cash Account", the availability and movement of special-purpose cash on special accounts in banks are taken into account.
57 Cash in transit Account 57 "Cash in transit" is intended to summarize information on cash flows in Belarusian rubles and foreign currencies deposited in banks, including through collection, or post offices for crediting to the settlement or other accounts of the organization, but not yet credited by appointment, as well as on the movement of funds in Belarusian rubles for the acquisition of foreign currency and cash in foreign currencies for sale.
57-1 Cash collected On subaccount 57-1 "Cash collected" takes into account the introduction of cash into the cash departments of banks, including through collection, or post offices and their crediting to the settlement or other accounts of the organization.
57-2 Cash for acquiring foreign currency Subaccount 57-2 "Cash for the acquisition of foreign currency" takes into account the movement of funds in Belarusian rubles for the acquisition of foreign currency. The debit of sub-account 57-2 “Cash for the acquisition of foreign currency” reflects the transfer of funds in Belarusian rubles for the purchase of foreign currency and the excess of the ruble equivalent of the purchased foreign currency over the amount in Belarusian rubles debited from the current account, and for the loan of the sub-account 57-2 "Cash for the acquisition of foreign currency" - the amount of purchased foreign currency at the purchase rate on the date of crediting to the foreign currency account and the excess of the amount debited from the current account to Belarus Sgiach rubles on ruble equivalent of the purchased foreign currency.
58 Short-term financial investments Account 58 "Short-term financial investments" is intended to summarize information on the presence and movement of investments in securities of other organizations, bonds of state and local loans (if the established maturity does not exceed 12 months), etc., as well as provided by the organization to other organizations loans (for a period of less than 12 months).
58-1 Short-term financial investments in securities In the account 58-1 "Short-term financial investments in securities" is kept a record of short-term investments in securities
58-2 Short-term loans granted Sub-account 58-2 “Short-term loans granted” keeps records of short-term loans granted
59 Impairment provisions for short-term financial investments Account 59 "Provisions for the impairment of short-term financial investments" is intended for the publication of information on the demand for short-term financial investments made in accordance with the established procedure. 90 “Income and expenses from current activities” (if the organization is a professional participant in the securities market), 91 “Other income and expenses” (if the organization is not a professional participant in the securities market) and loan account 59 “Reserves impairment of short-term financial investments. ” A similar record is made when the amount of the created reserve is increased. (as amended by Resolution of the Ministry of Finance of December 20, 2012 N 77) It is believed that the creation of reserve accounts and the reservation of accounts in accordance with the requirements of account 59 “Reserves and expenses for current activities” (in the case of 91 “Other income and expenses” (in case the organization is not a professional participant in the securities market). (as amended by Resolution of the Ministry of Finance of December 20, 2012 N 77) Analytical accounting for account 59 "Provisions for the impairment of short-term financial investments" is maintained for each reserve.
